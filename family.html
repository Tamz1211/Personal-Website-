My Family Story

I was raised by a strong and selfless woman — my mother. As a single mother, she gave everything to raise me and my elder brother with love, discipline, and strength. She's my role model, the reason I strive to be better every day.

My brother and I may not have always had the closest bond growing up. We fought, we disagreed, but deep down, he's someone I admire and care for more than I often show. He’s completed his degree and is now working towards his dreams, just like me.

And then there's Blu, my husky — more than a pet, he's my best friend. He understands my silence, celebrates my happiness, and stays by my side no matter what. In a world that’s always changing, Blu is my constant. My family may be small, but their love is what carries me forward every single day.

"In every success I earn, there’s a piece of them beside me."
